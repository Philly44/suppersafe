Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 2024

SupperSafe ("we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information.

What We Collect

We collect only the information necessary to provide our service:

  • Email address - When you join our waitlist or sign up for alerts
  • Referral data - Your referral code and who referred you (if applicable)

We do not collect names, addresses, payment information, or browsing history.

How We Use Your Information

  • To send you launch updates and service notifications
  • To send restaurant health inspection alerts (when you subscribe)
  • To track referrals and waitlist position

We will never sell your email address or share it with third parties for marketing purposes.

Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely using Supabase, a trusted database provider with encryption at rest and in transit. We use industry-standard security practices to protect your information.

Data Retention

We retain your email address until you unsubscribe or request deletion. You can do either at any time.

Your Rights

Under Canadian privacy law (PIPEDA), you have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your information
  • Unsubscribe from communications at any time

Cookies

We use localStorage to remember your waitlist status and referral code. We do not use tracking cookies or third-party analytics that identify you personally.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this policy or want to exercise your privacy rights, contact us at hello@suppersafe.com.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We'll notify you of significant changes via email.

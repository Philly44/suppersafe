What SupperSafe Does

SupperSafe provides information about restaurant health inspections in Toronto. We aggregate publicly available data from Toronto Public Health's DineSafe program and present it in an accessible format.

Data Source

All inspection data comes from Toronto Public Health's DineSafe program. This is the same data displayed on inspection notices in restaurant windows. We do not conduct our own inspections.

We update our database regularly, typically within hours of new inspections being published. However, there may occasionally be delays.

Not Official Health Guidance

  • Does not replace official inspection notices or health advisories
  • Should not be your sole basis for health or safety decisions

For official information, visit Toronto Public Health DineSafe.

Safety Scores

The safety scores we display are calculated by SupperSafe based on inspection data. They are our interpretation of the public record and are meant to help you quickly understand a restaurant's inspection history. They are not official ratings from Toronto Public Health.
